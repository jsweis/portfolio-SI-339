Professional Career

Teacher's Assistant

My first job I ever had was working as a teacher's assitant at my temple. For work, I helped prepare students for their bar or bat mitzvahs. I helped them learn to read Hebrew and chant prayers. I started working their my freshman year of high school and worked through my senior year.

Mathnasium

My next job was at a math learning center called Mathnasium. I worked here starting my junior year and worked until I left for college. I started as an instructor where I taught elementary through high school math, but was then promoted to a lead instructor where I trained new employees and spoke with parents about the program.

Homrun Group

Now, my upcoming internship for this summer will be in Tel Aviv, Israel working for Homrun group. This company helps Israeli start ups get their foot into the North American Market. This summer I will be working as an analyst helping conducting market research for future sales deals.
