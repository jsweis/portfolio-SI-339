School

Lower and Middle School

My parents made the decision to move to Westlake Village as it had a great public school system. Thus, I started my schooling journey at Westlake Hills Elementary School. Here I learned the basics setting me up for future success. After graduating elementary school, I moved onto Colina Middle School where I stayed for the next three years.

High School

After my early years, I arrived at Westlake High School. This is where school became real as my grades and extracirriculars started to matter for college. I worked hard in high school, taking honors and AP classes as well as being involved in sports, school clubs, and outside volunteering. It was a great four years, and although it was a lot of work, it was worth it and the friends I made there will definitely last a lifetime.

College

Now to college. The University of Michigan, the greatest school on the planet. I was scared at first to come here as I knew no one, it was very far from home, and I had to experience an actual winter. However, I could not be happier. I am involved in a social fraternity as well as a technology consulting group called Reach. It has been an extremely fast two years, and I cannot wait to see what the next two have to offer.
